About the band

Retrograde Reality is a one-man band formed by Brandon Frie in 2017. Brandon brings in guest performers from time to time to add their signature sound to his compositions.

Originally from Phoenix, AZ, Brandon now lives and records in South Texas. He has been playing music since the mid-80s when he first picked up a saxophone. He's since added keyboards, guitars, drums, and other percussion instruments to his collection.

In 2013, Brandon began recording and producing music in his home studio. He released his first original work, "Ain't Gonna Take It" shortly after putting together his studio. Afterwards, he produced mostly cover songs as he learned to record and mix music. He released several other songs over the years and continues to write and record original music.
